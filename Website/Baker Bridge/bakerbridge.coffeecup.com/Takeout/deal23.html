The bidding has been as shown. It is your bid. What do you say?

The redouble has taken the force from you - you can pass if you want to. But if you pass, the most likely scenario is that West will also pass. So partner will either have to let them play 1♦ redoubled (which will be a disaster for your side) or take out his own double by bidding some suit. So he will bid a suit and it will almost surely be 1♠.

You can help your side the most by bidding 1♥ immediately. You don't have to worry that partner will believe you have a good hand; both he and West have 13+ points and East's redouble shows 10 or more. He knows there are no points remaining for you to have.

On this deal your ♥ bid gets passed around to East who bids 2♦, the final contract.
