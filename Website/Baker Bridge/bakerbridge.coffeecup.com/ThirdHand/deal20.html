Put yourself in partner's shoes.
He almost surely has ♦ K Q 10 x (x).
When South holds up on his ♦A, which he almost surely will, West may think he is holding up with ♦ A J x, and be afraid to continue the suit.

You can help your partner figure out the true situation by merely playing your ♦J so he can see that South doesn't have it.

With this good play West will be able to continue ♦s and defeat the contract.
