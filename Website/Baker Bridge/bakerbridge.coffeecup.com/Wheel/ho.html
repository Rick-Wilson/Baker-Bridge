After you arrange your hand into suits, the next thing you do is count points.

    INITIAL POINT COUNTING
  • Ace=4   King=3   Queen=2   Jack=1
  • Add 1 point for any 5-card suit, 2 for a 6-card suit etc
  • Do not count doubletons, singletons or voids when deciding whether to open the bidding
    BIDDING GOAL : SUIT PRIORITIES
  1. Try to find an 8+ card fit in a Major suit; ♠s or ♥s
  2. Try to play a Notrump contract
  3. Try to find a 9+ card fit in a Minor suit; ♦s or ♣s
    BIDDING GOAL : CONTRACT LEVEL (total partnership points)
  • 25 points or below : partscore
  • 26 to 32 points : game in Notrump or Major suit
  • 29 to 32 points : game in a Minor suit
  • 33 to 36 points : small slam (12 tricks)
  • 37 points or above : Grand slam (13 tricks)


This method of counting points is followed throughout these pages.
