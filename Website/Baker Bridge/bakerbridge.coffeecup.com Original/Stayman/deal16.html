Partner bids 2NT. What is your next bid?

Partner would never bid Stayman without a 4-card Major. Since he did not like your ♥ bid it is clear that his 4-card Major must be ♠s.
His 2NT bid was an invitation for you to bid 3NT, which means he has about 9 points.
Knowing that partner has 9 points and 4 ♠s, your call is clear : 4♠.
