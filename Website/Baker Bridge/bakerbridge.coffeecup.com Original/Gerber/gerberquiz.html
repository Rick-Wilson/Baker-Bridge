HOW TO TAKE THE QUIZ

On a piece of paper write the numbers 1 through 12.
Examine each Bidding problem carefully.
If the final 4♣ bid is Gerber write YES by the appropriate number.
If the final 4♣ bid is not Gerber write NO by the number.

When you have written answers to all 12 problems go to the Answer page.
